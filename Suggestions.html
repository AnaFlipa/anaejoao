Suggestions

What to do

Eat Francesinha

A Portuguese sandwich originally from Porto, made with bread, a lot of meat and beer sauce.
There is also the vegan Francesinha at Francesinhas da Baixa.

Wine tasting

If your looking for some wine culture you can always visit the Sandeman or Calém cellars and enjoy Port wine in a unique environment.
Bridges cruise

Visit the 6 bridges and enjoy the surroundings while on a very traditional boat. Dont forget your sunscreen, hat and sunglasses!

Local Culture

When in Porto, do as "Portuenses" do. Explore the museums, parks, churches, monuments,

Beach

From north to south you can always enjoy the sand and the waves of a wild sea. Have you ever surfed? Now it's your chance.
Nightlife

Don't waste your nights and head to Galerias, where we learned how to dance Forró.
Perhaps you'll find the dancer in you!

Senhor de Matosinhos Fair

May to June
Huge market with lots of Dolce & Banana brands, carousels, fireworks and food like the famous "Farturas" (the big version of churros).
